My Favorite Yam mixed with Eggs

ALT School ID : ALT/SOE/025/3907

This is a simple recipe for a delicious dish that fries boiled yam and eggs together.

Recipe Information:

  • Prepation time: 20 minutes
  • Cooking time: 30 minutes
  • Servings: 5-8 slices of yam
  • Difficulty: Beginner

Ingredients:

  • 1 tuber of yam
  • 3 eggs (pending on how fuzzy you want the dish to be)
  • 2 cup of vegetable oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Species: pepper, onions, and other spices
  • 1-2 maggi cubes
  • Chopped onions and other vegetables
  • Crayfish for taste variety (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel the yam and cut it into thin slices.
  2. Boil the yam slices in salted water until tender.
  3. While the yam is boiling, chop the onions alongside the other vegetables.
  4. In a bowl, beat the eggs and season with salt, maggi, onions and pepper.
  5. Once the yam is boiled, drain it and set aside.
  6. In a separate bowl, mix the boiled yam slices with the beaten eggs and spices.
  7. Heat the vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  8. Add the boiled yam slices now mixed with the egg and spices to the pan and fry until golden brown.
  9. Pour the beaten eggs over the yam and cook until the eggs are set.
  10. Serve hot with curstard or oat.

Cooking Tips:

  • Ensure the yam is well boiled not too soft to avoid breaking during frying.
  • Adjust the amount of spices according to your taste preference.
  • For a richer flavor, you can add crayfish or other seafood.
